The Alloy Six

The Band

The Alloy Six has emerged from the pieces of Stockholm based The Moving Sounds and The Fourtune-Tellers.

The aim is to play rock 'n' roll influenced by the late 60s and early 70s music scene. The sound is fuelled by hammond organ and 12-string guitar, along with razor sharp distortion, solid drumming and spot-on bass.
